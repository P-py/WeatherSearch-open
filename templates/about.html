What is WeatherSearch?

WeatherSearch is a web-app developed by P-py based on python, including one of it's frameworks, flask, and the fundamentals of web development: Bootstrap-CSS and HTML5.

The app uses the OpenWeatherMap API to collect weather rellated infos of a specific location defined by the users input, and displays it for the user. Finally, making it easier to know more about the weather!

To know more deeply about the project, go click on the "Project" link on the top navigation bar.
